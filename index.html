--- Work ---

Research //

Sarah has worked at OHSU's in the Casey Eye Institute Elks Informatics lab for the past 4 years. Her research focuses on finding innovative ways to use retro active data captured in Electronic Health Record systems to drive meaningful changes in health care efficiency. Sarah's published works can be found on the NIH website.
